Industrial Separation

Recycling isn't sorting by hand—it's high-speed mechanical triage. Shredders break devices into 20mm fragments, and magnets pull steel away from plastics and copper.

The Shredder Chain

1

Primary Shred

Device is pulverized into large fragments. Batteries must be removed before this step to prevent fires.

2

Magnetic Triage

Overhead magnetic belts lift steel and iron fragments, isolating the ferrous material stream instantly.

3

Eddy Current

Rotating magnetic fields "eject" non-magnetic metals like aluminum and copper from the remaining plastic stream.
